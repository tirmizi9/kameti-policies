 # Privacy Policy for Kameti App

  **Last Updated:** January 2025

  **Effective Date:** January 2025

  Fast Web Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Kameti mobile application (the "App"). This
  Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use
  our App.

  ## 1. Information We Collect

  ### 1.1 Personal Information
  We collect the following personal information when you use our App:

  - **Phone Number:** Required for account registration and authentication via OTP verification
  - **Display Name:** Optional, for personalization within the app
  - **Email Address:** Optional, for account recovery and notifications
  - **Profile Information:** Optional details like occupation, WhatsApp number

  ### 1.2 Financial Information
  - **Kameti/Committee Records:** Details of rotating savings groups you create or join
  - **Payment Records:** Records of contributions and payouts within your kametis
  - **Member Information:** Names and contact details of kameti members you add

  ### 1.3 Device Information
  - **Device Identifiers:** For app functionality and security
  - **Device Model and OS Version:** For compatibility and troubleshooting
  - **App Version:** For updates and support

  ### 1.4 Usage Data
  - **App Analytics:** How you interact with the app (screens visited, features used)
  - **Crash Reports:** Technical data when the app encounters errors

  ## 2. How We Use Your Information

  We use the collected information for:

  - **Account Management:** Creating and managing your account
  - **Core Functionality:** Managing kametis, tracking payments, sending reminders
  - **Notifications:** Sending payment reminders and important updates
  - **Security:** Protecting against unauthorized access and fraud
  - **Improvement:** Analyzing usage to improve the app experience
  - **Support:** Responding to your inquiries and providing customer service

  ## 3. Data Storage and Security

  ### 3.1 Storage
  - Your data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore
  - Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  - Sensitive data is stored using device secure storage

  ### 3.2 Security Measures
  - Phone number verification (OTP) for authentication
  - Encrypted data transmission
  - Secure device storage for sensitive information
  - Regular security audits and updates

  ## 4. Data Sharing

  We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  - **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly agree
  - **Service Providers:** Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage
  - **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights
  - **Business Transfers:** In case of merger or acquisition (with notice to you)

  ### Third-Party Services We Use:
  - **Firebase Authentication:** For secure phone login
  - **Firebase Firestore:** For data storage
  - **Firebase Cloud Messaging:** For push notifications
  - **Firebase Analytics:** For usage analytics (anonymized)

  ## 5. Your Rights

  You have the right to:

  - **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
  - **Correction:** Update or correct your information
  - **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
  - **Portability:** Export your kameti and payment data
  - **Opt-out:** Disable optional notifications

  ## 6. Data Retention

  - **Active Accounts:** Data retained while your account is active
  - **Deleted Accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  - **Payment Records:** May be retained for 7 years for legal/tax purposes
  - **Analytics Data:** Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

  ## 7. Children's Privacy

  Our App is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from
   children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

  ## 8. International Data Transfers

  Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Pakistan where our service
  providers (Google/Firebase) operate. These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards.
